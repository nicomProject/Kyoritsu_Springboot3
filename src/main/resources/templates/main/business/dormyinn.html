Dormitory business

（Student Dormitories・Employee dormitories)

Secure housing with our devotion to the spirit of "welcome home"
Since the foundation, Kyoritsu Maintenance Co., Ltd. has run student dormitories/employee dormitories businesses. Currently, the scope of dormitories has expanded to international exchange dormitories, various theme-based dormitories and adaptations to a wide range of styles, with over 500 dormitories throughout Japan.

The major features of the student dormitories that we run are the "handmade home cooking" and the "regularly stationed dormitory leader couple." Each breakfast and dinner menu devised by a registered dietitian is handmade at respective dormitories. The dormitory leader couple supports student life by taking students to hospitals during medical events or giving advice on personal matters.
