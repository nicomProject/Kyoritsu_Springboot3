Management Philosophy

경영이념/비전

회사의 철학은 고객을 최우선으로 생각하는 것입니다.

01

Be thankful and be appreciated

Work in a way to be able to always say “thank you” to customers and, at the same time,
always have customers say “thank you” to you.

02

Gather wisdom and work to improve the service

Gather wisdom (knowledge that leads to action) from inside and outside the company,
and always work hard to make improvements.

03

Always desire for prosperity and work to achieve mutual profits

“Mutual profits” are essential to establish “Kyoritsu = rising up together.”
Contribute to the prosperity of clients by constantly providing satisfying services.
To achieve this, pursue co-existence and mutual profits with business associates and cooperating companies.
